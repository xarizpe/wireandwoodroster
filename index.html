We do not hide the machine; we use it to amplify the human ghost. The Wire and Wood Roster is a collection of distinct musical personas created through a synthesis of human lyrical concepts and AI generation. We craft the soul; the algorithm builds the sound.

The Roster is a set of personas, not a mask.

Each release begins with a human idea—lyrics, emotional intent, and narrative structure—then moves through a generative interpretation layer. We curate the output until it sounds like a real room, a real voice, a real moment.
